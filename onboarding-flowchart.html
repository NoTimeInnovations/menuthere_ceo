Petpooja Integration Flow

Only for Petpooja customers · menu auto-syncs from POS
Path: YES branch
Step 2.1 · Collect Details

Collect Petpooja details

Petpooja Restaurant ID Petpooja Registered Email Google Maps Location
Tax type?

Backward Tax — prices already include GST; no extra tax at checkout.

Forward Tax — GST calculated separately during billing.

Menu type?

Online Menu or Offline Menu

Most restaurants use Offline Menu.

Step 2.2 · Create + Email

Create restaurant in Menuthere

Inside Super Admin → Create Petpooja Partner → enter the collected details → Submit.

Email + website creation happens automatically on submit.
Step 2.3 · Wait for Approval

Wait for Petpooja approval

The Petpooja partner team reviews the request.

While waiting: maintain status in Excel and contact the Petpooja point-of-contact to speed it up.
Step 2.5 · Menu Sync

Menu synchronization

Once approved, Petpooja pushes the menu to Menuza. Verify everything synced correctly:

  • Categories · Items · Variants
  • Prices · Tax · Availability
  • Images
Step 2.6 · Image Creation

Food image creation

After the menu syncs, generate food images via Get All Images in Menu Management. Upload optimized images if the customer asks.

Images that didn't come through from Petpooja are filled in here, so the menu looks complete before the customer reviews it.
Step 2.7 · Customer Verification

Customer verifies the imported menu

Share the imported menu. Customer checks: Prices · Names · Categories · Availability · Taxes · Spellings.

Ask the customer to place a test order first: this confirms the tax is calculated correctly and the menu/pricing is right end-to-end before anything else. Fix any tax or price mismatch now.
After the test order, ask the customer to rearrange the menu (important): highest-selling items, most profitable items, chef recommendations and signature dishes should appear on top.

Order of this step: ① verify details → ② place a test order to check tax & menu → ③ rearrange the menu.

  • After approval, the Petpooja process ends
  • Send the customer their credentials + partner app links

Delivery Configuration — Choose Model

Pick how deliveries are fulfilled
Decision

Which delivery model fits the restaurant?

Option 1 · V1 — Own riders

Restaurant has its own riders. Created in Petpooja, then same riders added in Menuza.

Name & Phone Number must match exactly across both systems.

Order flow:

  • Customer orders
  • Restaurant accepts → dispatches
  • Manager selects rider
  • Rider gets notification → accepts
  • Delivery completed
Option 2 · V2 — Own + community

Restaurant wants own riders and community riders.

  • Delivery boys register themselves
  • Nearby riders within 5 KM get notified
  • Interested rider accepts
  • Delivery starts
Option 3 · Third-Party

No riders & no interest in V2 → enable Porter / Rapido.

  • Developer Abhin configures integration
  • Restaurant connects account
  • Restaurant periodically recharges → reconnect after
  • Verify successful integration

11

Customer Testing Phase

Customer places real test orders to confirm delivery & tax rules work
Testing · Goal

Ask the customer to test the live setup

Before marketing materials and go-live, have the customer place real test orders and confirm each of these behaves correctly:

① Delivery Radius

Order from inside the radius → should be accepted.

Order from outside the radius → should be blocked / show "out of range".

② Delivery Charges

Confirm the delivery fee shown at checkout matches the configured charge for the distance / order value.

③ Tax (Petpooja, if any)

Confirm tax from Petpooja is applied correctly at checkout.

Backward tax → no extra tax added. Forward tax → GST shown separately. Skip if the restaurant has no tax.

Do not proceed to go-live until delivery radius, delivery charges, and tax all behave correctly in a real test order. Re-check Phase 9 settings if anything is off.
  • Test order inside radius accepted
  • Test order outside radius blocked
  • Delivery charge correct at checkout
  • Petpooja tax correct at checkout (if applicable)
